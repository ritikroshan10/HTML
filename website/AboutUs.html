Achievements

Achievements in life can be defined as actions that have a positive impact on an individual and their profession. These can include personal accomplishments, career milestones, and experiences that demonstrate technical skill, dedication, and perseverance.
When discussing achievements in life, it's essential to be specific, focusing on the skills and experiences that are relevant to the context. Using the STAR method ( Situation, Task, Action, Result) can be helpful in structuring a response to questions about achievements. Ultimately, achievements in life are a personal and subjective concept, and what constitutes an achievement can vary greatly from person to person.

our journey

In 2004, fellow MIT graduate students Brian Halligan and Dharmesh Shah noticed a major shift in the way people shop and purchase products. Buyers didn't want to be interrupted by ads, they wanted helpful information.

In 2006, they founded HubSpot to help companies use that shift to grow better with inbound marketing into a crafted, not cobbled suite of products that create the frictionless customer experience that buyers expect today.
